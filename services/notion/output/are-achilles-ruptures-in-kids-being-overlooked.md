## Content overview


### **Are Achilles Ruptures In Kids Being Overlooked?**


Achilles tendon rupture is RARE in pediatric patients, but when it happens, diagnosis is often **missed 20–30% of the time** on exam.


A new case series highlights how **POCUS (point-of-care ultrasound)** can change the game:

- 👦 First reported use of POCUS for pediatric Achilles rupture
- 🩺 Both cases were correctly diagnosed at the bedside
- 🚑 Faster recognition **→** earlier management and improved outcomes

If POCUS is this effective in adults, should it now be standard for **pediatric sports injuries** too?


Source: [https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/34550919/](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/34550919/)
