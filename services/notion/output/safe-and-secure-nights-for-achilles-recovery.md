Thank you, MP4, for sharing your experience. 🌙


Security matters the most during Achilles recovery. Re-rupture is the worst outcome, so we focus on making a splint that keeps patients safe and comfortable at night. 🔒🦶


MP4 highlighted two things we work hard on:

- It holds the foot at the right angle for healing📐
- The straps stay secure during sleep🌛

We are proud to offer a solid alternative to the heavy boot for nighttime rest. Safety and comfort should always work together.💪✨
