## Close the gap to speed the heal.


Early recovery is about one thing: **Tendon Approximation.**


Neutral positioning pulls the tendon ends apart.


Pointing the toes down brings them together.


Closer ends mean a stronger, tighter repair.


Don't rush the stretch.


Protect the length.


**Keep the ends close. Keep them protected.** ⏱️
