### The Achilles Sleep Struggle


Boots are heavy, hot, and bulky.


But 24/7 protection is non-negotiable.


Night splints are a lighter, breathable alternative.


They keep your foot pointed down while you rest.


Crucial: Never sleep "barefoot" in the early phase.


Protect the knit. Protect your sleep.


Did you keep the boot on or switch to a splint? ⏱️
