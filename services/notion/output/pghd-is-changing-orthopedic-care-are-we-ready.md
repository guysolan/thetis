## Content overview


PGHD is changing orthopedic care—are we ready?

A recent study explored how patient-generated health data (PGHD) impacts orthopedic care.

Key insights:


– Improves motivation, education, and adherence 📈
– Adds workflow strain and concern over data quality ⚠️
– Clinicians see opportunities with better dashboards and personalized rehab 🖥️



How do we integrate patient data without overwhelming providers?


Do you use PGHD in your practice?
