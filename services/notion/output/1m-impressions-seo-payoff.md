Our hard work writing SEO articles is paying off


## 
🔎 **Over 1.01M impressions and 18.1K clicks — what are Achilles patients really searching for?**


Key insights:

- Search intent remains highly practical: recovery timelines, when walking resumes, and splint management
- Noticeable spikes follow new educational articles and real patient stories
- Average CTR: **1.8%**, with most users engaging deeply with long form recovery guides

**What this tells us:**


Patients are not searching for generic advice or quick fixes. They are actively looking for **clarity, reassurance, and realistic recovery expectations**, especially during the early and most uncertain phases of rehabilitation.


This reinforces the value of **evidence based education** that explains not just _what_ to do, but _why_ recovery timelines and protection strategies differ between patients.
