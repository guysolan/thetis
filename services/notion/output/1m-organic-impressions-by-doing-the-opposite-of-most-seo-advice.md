## Content overview
1M organic impressions by doing the opposite of most SEO advice.


Over the past 12 months, Thetis Medical’s educational content reached:

- **1.01M organic impressions**
- **18.1K clicks from search**
- **Average position ~11**, with steady, sustained visibility

This wasn’t driven by high volume or automated content.



Our articles are written or **clinically vetted by Foot and Ankle experts**, designed to answer real patient and clinician 
questions clearly and accurately. We avoid generic AI generated summaries and prioritise clinical relevance over speed.



- It’s a slower approach.
- It requires more expert input.
- And results take time to compound.



When content is genuinely useful and evidence led, it becomes one of the most reliable long term growth channels for both clinicians and patients.
