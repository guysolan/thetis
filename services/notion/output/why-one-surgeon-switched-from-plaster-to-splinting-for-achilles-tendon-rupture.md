## Content overview


A 42-year-old runner🏃‍♂️ with an acute Achilles tendon rupture was initially treated with a traditional plaster cast. Recovery was slow, mobility was limited, and follow-up visits were frequent⏳.


We introduced a functional splint with wedge support:

- **Early controlled motion** ⚡
- **Reduced stiffness** 💪
- **Faster return to weight-bearing** ⏱️


The patient regained mobility **20% faster** and reported higher comfort 😌.

👩‍⚕️👨‍⚕️Surgeons, physiotherapists — what’s your preferred rehab timeline for Achilles tendon rupture patients? Do you use splints over plaster?
