**Achilles Surgery Recovery: When to Start Scar Care**


Around **2 weeks after Achilles surgery**, patients typically attend a wound check and suture removal appointment.


Once the wound is **fully closed** with no open areas or scabs, gentle scar care can begin.


Scar massage can help keep tissue supple and improve mobility around the incision.


Simple approach:

- Use a moisturiser or oil to reduce friction
- Apply **light pressure only**
- Massage in **small circular movements**
- Spend **5 to 10 minutes, 2 to 3 times per day**

Consistency matters more than the product used.
