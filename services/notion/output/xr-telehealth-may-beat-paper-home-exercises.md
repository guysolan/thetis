## Content overview


XR + Telehealth may beat paper home exercises 🦾

A pilot study tested Tele-PhyT, an extended reality telerehab system, for joint arthroplasty patients.


Findings:


– Provided real-time visual feedback 👀
– Smooth full-body tracking 
– Clinicians and patients preferred it over paper programs ✅


Do you think XR systems could improve adherence and outcomes in rehab, or are they still too far from daily practice?

Source: [https://arxiv.org/abs/2502.14059](https://arxiv.org/abs/2502.14059)
