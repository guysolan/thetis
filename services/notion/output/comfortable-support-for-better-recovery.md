Material is good quality and offers proper support, maintaining your ankle stable as long as it's the right fit. Since it's not bulky, it doesn't get in the way of sleeping, which is great. Thank you, Carlos, for the honest feedback. 🌙


We made our Achilles rupture night splint easy to put on and take off because patients move between the boot and the splint many times each day. It must be quick to use, even during late night trips to the bathroom.


Small details like these help patients rest better and feel more confident while healing.


If you treat **Achilles tendon injuries**, let’s connect.
