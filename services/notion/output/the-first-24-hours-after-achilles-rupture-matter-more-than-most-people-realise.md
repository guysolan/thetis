## The First 24 Hours After Achilles Rupture Are About Protection, Not Progress


After an Achilles rupture, the early phase is not about exercises or decisions. It is about protection.


The single most important principle in the first days is maintaining the tendon in a protected position. Poor early positioning can influence tendon length and long term strength.


Clear early guidance should include:

- Immediate immobilisation
- Clear weight bearing instructions
- Education about red flag symptoms
- Timely specialist follow up

Early clarity reduces anxiety and prevents avoidable setbacks.
