## Content overview


**The Hidden Risk After Achilles Surgery: What Happens While You Sleep**


Achilles tendon re-rupture remains one of the most concerning complications after repair, with studies reporting rates from **2% to 8%**, depending on rehabilitation protocols.


Recent clinical evidence suggests that **controlled early motion** combined with **targeted protection** — especially during unsupervised periods like sleep — may improve outcomes.


The **Achilles Rupture Splint** provides that missing link: a balance between protection and comfort during the critical healing phase.


⚙️ Maintains functional resting position


🛏️ Enables safer recovery at night


🌿 Supports better compliance through comfort and convenience


As we continue to integrate evidence-based devices into rehabilitation, preventive design becomes not just a feature — but a responsibility.


In your experience, what’s the most overlooked factor in post-op Achilles rehab — protection, comfort, or compliance?
