## Content overview


Strengthening Achilles Care Across Canada


We’re pleased to announce that **Ortho Active** is now the **exclusive Canadian distributor** of the **Thetis Achilles Splint** — expanding their national Achilles-care portfolio alongside the OPED VACOped.


This partnership gives clinicians across Canada access to a **complete functional-rehab pathway**, combining the best walking performance, with the best night time comfort.


Here’s why this matters 👇


**Achilles ruptures are rising in Canada**, but the latest developments in Achilles rupture equipment are yet to reach most patients.


**Enpowered by the VACOped boot and the Thetis Splint, Ortho Active** are now in a position to change this and give ATR patients in Canada the gold standard of treatment.


We’re excited to see the word spread and the patients recovery experience improve, day and night.
