## Content overview


**Surgery or Conservative Care: What’s the Lesser Evil for Achilles Ruptures?**


A 2023 systematic review of **34 meta-analyses** breaks down the trade-offs clinicians face:

- 🔁 **Re-rupture rates:**

    Surgical: **2.3–5%**


    Conservative: **3.9–13%**

- ⚠️ **Complications:**

    ↑↑ Higher with surgery (infections, sural nerve injury)


    ↓Lower with conservative care

- ✂️ **Open vs MIS repair:** Similar re-rupture rates, but MIS linked to fewer complications
- 🦵 **Rehab timing:** No clear difference between early vs late protocols

👉 So, **is lower re-rupture worth higher complication risk?**


source: [https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/37115231/](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/37115231/)
