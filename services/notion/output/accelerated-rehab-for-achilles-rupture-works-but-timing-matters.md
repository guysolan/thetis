## Accelerated Rehab for Achilles Rupture Works—But Timing Matters ⏱


A latest meta-analysis of 43 Randomized Controlled Trials (2,553 patients) highlights the power of accelerated rehab for Achilles ruptures. Results show re-rupture rates vary by protocol type:


– Early immobilization: 4.07% re-rupture
– Early immobilization + **Artificial Rupture of Membranes**: 5.95%
– Early weight-bearing + **Artificial Rupture of Membranes**: 3.61%



Accelerated rehab (no immobilization): just 1.36%



Early motion and weight-bearing paired with active rehab protocols deliver the lowest re-rupture risk.
