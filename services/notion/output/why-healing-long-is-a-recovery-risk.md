## Instagram


**Why "Healing Long" is a recovery risk.**


A healthy tendon is a stiff, strong spring.


After rupture, the tissue becomes more compliant.


It stretches more easily under lower loads.


This is why we protect your foot position.


We load gradually to prevent permanent lengthening.


**Stiffness is strength. Protect your length.** ⏱️
