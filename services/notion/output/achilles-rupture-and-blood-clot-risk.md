## Achilles Rupture and Blood Clot Risk


Achilles rupture plus immobilisation increases the risk of venous thromboembolism.


Reduced walking, impaired calf pump function, and local inflammation all contribute.


Quick reminder:

- **DVT** = clot in the leg
- **PE** = clot in the lungs (emergency)

Red flags patients should understand:

- New calf pain
- One leg swelling
- Sudden breathlessness
- Chest pain or fainting

Clear early counselling matters.
