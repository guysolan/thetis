Would you fancy smelling someones feet who’ve worn the same shoes and socks for 12 weeks?


On top of the inconvenience of having to walk around in an orthopedic boot, let’s save patients the the awful, compounding **10-week Boot Stink**.


There are three little recommendations you can make to your patients which they, and their partner, will thank you for:

1. **Night Splint is Gold:** This keeps the tendon safe AND gives the heavy walking boot **8 hours of airing-out time** every night.
2. **Wool Socks are Heroes:** Forget cotton. Tell them to get Merino wool socks—they fight sweat and odor better than anything else.
3. **Powder Every Day:** Advise them to use antifungal powder daily. It soaks up moisture and stops the smell-causing bacteria from growing.
