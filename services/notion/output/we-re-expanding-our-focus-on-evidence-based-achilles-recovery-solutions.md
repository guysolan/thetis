## Content overview


**We’re Expanding Our Focus on Evidence-Based Achilles Recovery Solutions**


At **Thetis Medical**, our mission has always been to support clinicians treating Achilles tendon ruptures with tools that are **simple, evidence-based, and effective**.


This quarter, we’re expanding our efforts by focusing on two key areas:


⚙️ **Enhanced Clinical Integration** – strengthening collaboration with rehabilitation centers to better track real-world outcomes.


🧠 **Education & Awareness** – sharing data-driven insights through clinician-focused updates and research summaries.


These initiatives reflect our commitment to aligning innovation with **practical results** — helping clinicians make informed choices and improving recovery for every patient.


We believe progress in Achilles rupture care isn’t just about new products — it’s about **smarter application of existing knowledge** and **consistent clinical follow-up**.
