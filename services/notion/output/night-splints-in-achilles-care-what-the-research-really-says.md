## Content overview


**Night Splints in Achilles Care: What the Research Really Says**

Night splints have been explored more often in Achilles tendinopathy than in full ruptures — but the insights are still valuable for clinicians.



📈 A small study found night splints + exercise improved outcomes, but evidence remains limited.


🧩 Reviews show mixed results — relief yes, major gains unclear.


Still, clinicians often use them as an **adjunct tool** — to limit overnight dorsiflexion and reduce tendon strain during early healing. The takeaway? Night splints may not replace traditional rehab, but they can provide valuable **support during recovery**.


For the Thetis Medical Achilles rupture splint, our focus isn’t on rehab but on comfort. They say ‘sleep is the best medicine’ and we fully believe your tendon can’t heal if you can’t sleep.
