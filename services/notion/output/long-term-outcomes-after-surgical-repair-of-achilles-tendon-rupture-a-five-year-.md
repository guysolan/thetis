## Content overview


Long-Term Outcomes After Surgical Repair of Achilles Tendon Rupture: A Five-Year Follow-Up Study 🦶📊



A systematic review and meta-analysis published in 2025 examined the **long-term outcomes of surgical repair** for acute Achilles tendon rupture. 📈


The study included **six studies with 806 patients** and found that surgical repair demonstrated excellent long-term outcomes, including:

- ✅ **Low re-rupture rate**: 4.2%
- ✅ **Low revision rate**: 3.7%
- ⚡ **Uncommon contralateral ruptures**: 5 cases

**Postoperative complications:**

- 🩸 Deep vein thrombosis: 6.3%
- 🏥 Wound infections: 3.6%

These findings underscore the **reliability, durability, and overall success** of surgical intervention in effectively treating Achilles tendon ruptures over **five years**. 🏃‍♂️💪
