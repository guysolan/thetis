## What happens in the first 72 hours after an Achilles tendon rupture?


Early management decisions can significantly influence rehabilitation outcomes.


Many patients struggle with:

- Improper immobilisation positioning
- Sleep disruption during early recovery
- Delays in structured pathway support
- Inconsistent splint application in emergency settings

Night positioning and early stabilisation are not just comfort measures.


They are part of structured rehabilitation strategy.


At Thetis Medical, our focus is simple:


Support hospital pathways.


Improve patient positioning compliance.


Enhance early recovery experience.


Our Achilles night splint is designed specifically for:

- Secure immobilisation during sleep
- Consistent ankle positioning
- Reduced unnecessary strain during early healing

Because recovery should be structured. Not improvised.
