30% of Achilles rupture patients remove their boot to sleep!


If you treat ATRs, you’ll have heard patients complain about sleeping in their boot. However, those who just ignore advice and remove it probably never tell you.


Prescribing a dedicated **Achilles Rupture Night Splint** to maintain the necessary plantar-flexed position  while the patient rests will protect the healing tendon length without compromising sleep comfort! 


Let your patients be safe AND comfortable!
