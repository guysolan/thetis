## Achilles Rupture: Watch for Clots.


Immobilisation increases VTE risk.


Less walking = slower blood flow.


DVT: A clot in the leg.


PE: A clot in the lungs.


**Know the Red Flags:**


Breathlessness or chest pain.


New calf pain or one-leg swelling.


**Stay vigilant. Stay safe.** ⏱️
