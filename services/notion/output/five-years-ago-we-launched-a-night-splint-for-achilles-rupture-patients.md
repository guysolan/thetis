Five years ago, we launched a night splint for Achilles rupture patients.


Since then, we've helped over 5,000 patients get better rest while their tendon heals.


After making the splint, we spent a lot of time making resources patients could read to help with their recovery alongside the splint.


These resources are spread out over the internet. Patients often land on a detailed article about one topic, but never learn the basics of another.


Patients can't remember everything they hear from their clinician.


So we're releasing our second product: a digital course to guide patients through recovery.


Step by step. Insights from the latest ATR research. Medical principles. Practical tips.


---


**For clinicians:**


A resource you can point your patients to. A structured, evidence-based guide covering everything from emergency care to return to sport.


Your patients can focus on recovery, not googling at 2am wondering if that pain is normal.


You can focus on the questions that matter, not repeating information you've already given.


---


Five years ago, we started with a splint to solve one problem: helping patients sleep.


In the next five years we plan move from an Achilles Rupture Night Splint Company to an Achilles Rupture Company. This means more products to improve ruptures, and education is our first step.


The course launches soon.


If you're a clinician looking for resources to share with patients, drop a comment and we'll make sure you're first to know when it's live.
