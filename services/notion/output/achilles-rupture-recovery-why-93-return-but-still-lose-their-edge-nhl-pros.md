## Content overview


Just as professional athletes push past career-threatening Achilles injuries, so too must we strive for smarter treatment and support in clinical practice.


✨ **Highlights:**

- 📊 **NHL Achilles Return Rates Reveal a Glaring Gap**

    A study tracking NHL players from 2008–2024 found that **14 of 15 players (93 %)** returned to play after Achilles rupture, with a median recovery time of **197 days** (IQR 105)—yet **career length and performance often took a hit** post-injury.


    [Read study →](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC11607746/?utm_source=chatgpt.com)
