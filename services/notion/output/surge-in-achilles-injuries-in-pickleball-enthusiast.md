## Content overview


Surge in Achilles Injuries in Pickleball Enthusiasts



The rapid rise of pickleball, with nearly 20 million players in 2024, has been accompanied by a significant increase in Achilles tendon injuries, particularly among older players. Experts like Dr. James Gladstone and Dr. Timothy Charlton attribute this to intense play without proper warm-ups and the prioritization of style over safety. ⚠️


**Key Insights:**


– Pickleball's popularity is leading to more Achilles injuries 🏓


– Older players are particularly at risk 👴👵


– Proper warm-up and appropriate footwear are essential 👟


How can clinicians educate recreational athletes about the risks of Achilles injuries in sports like pickleball?🤔
