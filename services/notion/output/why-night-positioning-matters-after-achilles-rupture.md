## Content overview


Achilles rupture recovery does not stop when the patient goes to sleep.


Poor night positioning can:

- Increase discomfort
- Reduce compliance
- Disrupt structured rehabilitation
- Delay confidence in early mobility

Consistent immobilisation during rest is part of pathway discipline.


Thetis Medical develops splint solutions specifically for Achilles rupture care.


Focused.
Specialised.
Pathway aligned.


Recovery is not just about healing.
It is about structured support.
