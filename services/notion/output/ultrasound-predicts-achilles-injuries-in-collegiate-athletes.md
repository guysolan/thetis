## Content overview


Ultrasound Predicts Achilles Injuries in Collegiate Athletes



A recent study involving 695 NCAA Division I athletes found that B-mode ultrasound can identify precursors to Achilles tendon injuries, enabling early intervention and potentially reducing time-loss injuries.🩺


**Key Insights:**


– Ultrasound can detect early signs of Achilles tendon issues 🔍


– Early detection allows for timely intervention ⏱️


– This approach could reduce overall injury rates 📉


Should routine ultrasound screening become standard practice for athletes at risk of Achilles injuries?🤔
