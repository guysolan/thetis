## Content overview


**Is Ultrasound the Gold Standard for Diagnosing Achilles Ruptures?**


A systematic review + meta-analysis evaluated ultrasound against surgery as the reference standard. The results are hard to ignore:

- 🔍 **Sensitivity:** **94.8%** for detecting complete ruptures
- ✅ **Specificity:** **98.7%**
- 📈 **Positive likelihood ratio:** 74.0
- 📉 **Negative likelihood ratio:** 0.05
- ⚠️ Up to **20% of ruptures are still misdiagnosed** (often as ankle sprains)

👉 The authors suggest a negative ultrasound can effectively rule out rupture – complete _and_ partial.


Do you use ultrasound routinely, or still rely primarily on clinical exam for suspected ruptures?


source:[https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/34801318/](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/34801318/)
