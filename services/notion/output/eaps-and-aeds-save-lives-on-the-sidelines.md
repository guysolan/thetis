## EAPs and AEDs save lives on the sidelines ⚡

Team physicians in school and NCAA sports must be prepared. Practical guidance stresses:


– Have a written Emergency Action Plan (EAP) 📝
– Identify staff with ACLS/BLS training 🫀
– Locate and confirm access to AEDs 🔋
– Assign roles for equipment removal and communication 📢


Are EAPs and AEDs fully standard in your setting—or still overlooked?
