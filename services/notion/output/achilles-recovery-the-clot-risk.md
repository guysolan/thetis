## Achilles Recovery: The Clot Risk.


Immobilisation slows your blood flow.


This increases your risk of VTE.


Medication helps, but awareness saves lives.


**Watch for DVT:** Swelling, warmth, or new pain.


**Watch for PE (Emergency):** Breathlessness or chest pain.


**Prevention starts with knowing the signs.** ⏱️
