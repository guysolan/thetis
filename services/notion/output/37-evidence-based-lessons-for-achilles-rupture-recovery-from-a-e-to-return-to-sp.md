37 evidence-based lessons for Achilles Rupture Recovery. From A&E to return to sport.


Here’s complete structure:


Module 0: Practical Guides (Throughout Recovery)

1. Emergency Care & Initial Assessment
    - What just happened, A&E expectations, and immediate first steps
2. Pain Management Throughout Recovery
    - Understanding pain at different stages, when pain is normal vs concerning, and pain management strategies
3. Mental Health & Psychological Recovery
    - Managing frustration, anxiety, depression, and fear of re-injury during recovery
4. Driving After Achilles Rupture
    - When patients can drive, left vs right leg injury, automatic vs manual, and insurance considerations

Module 1: Emergency Phase (Days 0-3)

1. Blood Clot Prevention - Critical Information
    - Understanding DVT/PE risk and blood thinners

Module 2: Early Treatment (Days 2-14)

1. Week 1 Checklist & What's Normal
    - Normal vs concerning symptoms, questions for clinic, essential purchases
2. Choosing Your Walking Boot
    - Aircast vs VACOped comparison, essential equipment, and product recommendations
3. Your Specialist Appointment
    - What to expect at orthopaedics, physical examination, and imaging
4. Surgery vs Non-Surgical Treatment
    - Understanding options, UKSTAR trial findings, and making decisions

Module 3: Boot Phase (Days 14-56)

1. Your Walking Boot - The Foundation
    - Aircast vs VACOped, understanding how boots work, and boot basics
2. Boot Adjustment and Care
    - Fitting boot, straps, padding, maintenance, and preventing problems
3. Boot Progression Protocol
    - Wedge removal schedule, weight-bearing progression, and angle changes
4. Sleeping with Your Boot
    - Night splints, sleep strategies, and making nights bearable
5. Washing and Foot Hygiene
    - Keeping clean safely while protecting healing tendon
6. Crutches and Mobility Aids
    - Crutch comfort, EVENup, leg length difference, and making mobility easier
7. Understanding Your Healing Tendon
    - The proliferative phase, tendon length, and why patience matters
8. Nutrition for Tendon Healing
    - Protein requirements, collagen supplementation, vitamins, and hydration for optimal recovery
9. Wedge Removal Protocol
    - When and how to remove wedges, angle progression, and what to expect
10. Building Strength While in Boot
    - Phase 1 exercises, weight-bearing progression, and preparing for Phase 2
11. The Final Boot Phase
    - Remodelling phase, tendon strength, and removing final wedges

Module 4: Transition (Days 74-98)

1. Leaving Your Boot Behind
    - When it's safe, first steps in shoes, and choosing the right footwear
2. Scar Management After Boot Removal
    - Understanding scar tissue, scar massage, bio-oil, and when to start scar management
3. Managing Post-Boot Challenges
    - Stiffness, swelling, and why patients shouldn't stretch aggressively

Module 5: Physiotherapy (Days 63-105)

1. Starting Physiotherapy
    - First session expectations, early goals, and the importance of consistency
2. The 6 Best Exercises for This Phase
    - Towel stretch, heel raises, resistance bands, and balance training
3. Re-Learning to Walk Properly
    - Heel-to-toe pattern, correcting limping, and gait analysis
4. Progressive Strengthening Begins
    - Single-leg heel raises, eccentric drops, and resistance training

Module 6: Recovery (Days 84-160)

1. Swimming & Water Activities
    - When to start swimming, pool exercises, safety considerations, and benefits for recovery
2. Building Cardio Without Risk
    - Swimming, stationary bike, elliptical, and walking as exercise
3. The Functional Milestones
    - Pain-free walking, 25+ heel raises, balance tests, and stairs
4. Returning to Normal Life
    - Work, driving, household tasks, and nutrition for tendon health

Module 7: Advanced (Days 200-220)

1. Starting to Run Again
    - Walk-jog progression, proper technique, surface selection, and avoiding pitfalls
2. Plyometric Training and Jumping
    - When to start, progression from pogo jumps to sport-specific training

Module 8: Long-Term (Days 180-240)

1. The 6-Month Milestone
    - Where the tendon is now, return-to-sport criteria, and psychological readiness
2. Preventing Re-Rupture
    - Risk factors, ongoing strengthening, warm-up protocols, and warning signs
3. Life After Achilles Rupture
    - The tendon will always be different, managing expectations, and long-term care
4. When Things Don't Go to Plan
    - Not progressing, tendon elongation, and when to seek further opinion
