Would you fancy smelling someones feet who’ve worn the same shoes and socks for 12 weeks?


Let’s be real, the enemy of Achilles recovery isn't just pain—it’s the awful, compounding **10-week Boot Funk**.


The simple, non-negotiable tip we share with clinicians is to **fight the smell before your patient cheats**. Proactively tackling the funk is the absolute key to keeping them compliant **24/7**. We know a smelly boot means they're compliant, but a frustrated patient is someone who will risk a quick, dangerous "air-out."


That single, quick moment of relief can cause a dangerous ankle bend and risk a re-rupture. Don't let a bad smell ruin the recovery! Here’s the light-hearted **Freshen-Up Plan** to help patients stick with the 'Boot Life':

- **Night Splint is Gold:** This keeps the tendon safe AND gives the heavy walking boot **8 hours of airing-out time** every night.
- **Wool Socks are Heroes:** Forget cotton. Tell them to get Merino wool socks—they fight sweat and odor better than anything else.
- **Powder Every Day:** Advise them to use antifungal powder daily. It soaks up moisture and stops the smell-causing bacteria from growing.

Don't let the boot funk undo your hard work.
