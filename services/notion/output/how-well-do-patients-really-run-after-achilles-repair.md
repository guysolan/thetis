## How Well Do Patients Really Run After Achilles Repair?


Return to sport is often reported as a yes/no outcome.


This large U.S. military study looked deeper — at **measured running performance** 🏃‍♂️


In over **1,000 U.S. Navy service members** who underwent Achilles tendon repair:


⏱️ **1.5-mile run times worsened** by ~41 seconds after surgery


📉 **69% of runners** maintained or improved their fitness category post-repair


🔁 **Running performance improved over time**, even beyond 1 year


🚴 Cycling performance was less affected than running


Importantly, **first-time fitness test failure was rare (≤1.5%)**, highlighting functional resilience in active populations.


**Clinical question:** How do you counsel runners on performance expectation, not just return-to-activity?
