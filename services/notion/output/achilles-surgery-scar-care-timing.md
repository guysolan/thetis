## Instagram


**Achilles Surgery: Scar Care Timing**


Wound check usually happens at Week 2.


Wait for full closure before touching the scar.


No open areas. No scabs.


Use oil or moisturiser for smooth movement.


**Light pressure only.**


Small circular motions to break down tissue.


5–10 minutes. 3x per day.


**Consistency is the key to mobility.** ⏱️
