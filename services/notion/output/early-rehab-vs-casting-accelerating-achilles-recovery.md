## Content overview


The UKSTAR trial (_The Lancet_, 2024) tested early functional rehab in Achilles tendon rupture patients. Early mobilization led to faster recovery without extra complications.


Here’s what they found 


🏃‍♂️ **Recovery:** Faster return to daily activities


🩺 **Safety:** No increase in complications


 Early rehab may challenge traditional casting methods. Patient selection is key to ensure safety and optimal outcomes.


Would you implement early functional rehab in your practice? Why or why not?
