The Achilles Boot Rule Most Patients Learn Too Late


Lowering your heel is a science.


Too fast = Tendon elongation.


Too slow = Stiff joints.


The Golden Timeline:


Wk 0–5: Max pointed down.


Wk 5+: First wedge removed.


Wk 10: Transition to shoes.


Let the tissue adapt. Don't rush the neutral. ⏱️
