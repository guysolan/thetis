## Content overview


Clinical Tip: Keys to Safe Non-Operative Rehabilitation for Achilles Rupture


If you manage Achilles ruptures non-operatively, consider this evidence-based rehab framework — proven to balance safety and recovery outcomes.

- ✅ **Early Weight-Bearing + Controlled Motion:** According to a 2021 review, starting WB and gentle motion early can lower time-to-return and reduce immobilization complications — without raising re-rupture risk.
- 🛡️ **Delay Orthosis Removal:** The same review warns against early orthosis removal — doing so increased re-rupture and delayed return to work.
- 📆 **Structured Follow-Up:** Regular functional assessments (heel-rise test, ATRS) to track progress and detect early signs of elongation or weakness.
- 🧑‍⚕️ **Patient Selection Matters:** Best for compliant patients, low-demand lifestyle, good vascular and tissue health. In high-demand or poor-compliance cases — reassess surgical vs non-operative risk/benefit.

**Takeaway:** Non-operative care + early controlled rehab can work — but only under careful protocols and clinical oversight.
