Amazon now shows an _Achilles Rupture Package_ when someone buys the Thetis Achilles splint.



The pack includes:

1. The VACOped, which may clinicians consider the gold standard boot for Achilles rupture
2. The Even-up, an extra sole to go on the non injured foot so when walking in the boot, both legs are the same length.
3. The Thetis Medical splint, which enables patients to safely remove the boot at night to get their sleep.

---


**UPDATE**


Clinicians, are amazon missing anything you like to recommend for Achilles rupture patients?



The bundle includes three items patients often end up needing:

- **VACOped boot**. Many clinicians view this as a leading option for Achilles rupture immobilization.
- **EvenUp shoe lift**. Helps keep both legs the same height while walking in a boot.
- **Thetis night splint**. Gives patients a safe option to remove the boot at night so they can sleep.

This is the first time Amazon has grouped these three together. It may reduce confusion for newly injured patients who ask, “What else do I need?”
