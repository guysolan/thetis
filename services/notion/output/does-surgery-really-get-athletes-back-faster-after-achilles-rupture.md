## Content overview


### **Does Surgery Really Get Athletes Back Faster After Achilles Rupture?**


A 2024 systematic review & meta-analysis explored **Return to Play (RTP)** and functional outcomes:

- 🏃‍♂️ **RTP rates:** No significant difference between **surgery vs. conservative** treatment
- 🔍 **Open repair vs. conservative:** No difference in RTP rates
- ⏱️ **Rehab timing:** Open repair + early rehab cut RTP time by **~4 weeks**
- 📈 **ATRS scores:** Similar regardless of surgical vs. conservative, or early vs. late rehab

👉 So if RTP rates are consistently high – should rehab timing be the bigger focus, not surgery vs conservative care?


**Source:** [https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/38296023/](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/38296023/)
