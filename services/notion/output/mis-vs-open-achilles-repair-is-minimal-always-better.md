## Content overview


MIS vs Open Achilles Repair Is Minimal Always Better? ⚖️


A recent Level I meta-analysis (522 patients) compared open repair to minimally invasive surgery (MIS) for Achilles ruptures. Findings:


– Re-rupture rates: 2.5% open vs 1.5% MIS equivalent
– Wound infection: 6.0% open vs 0.4% MIS statistically significant
– Sural nerve injury: 3.4% MIS, none in open cases


What’s your take favor MIS for lower infection risk, or open repair for avoiding nerve complications?
