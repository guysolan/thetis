Early Rehabilitation After Achilles Rupture: Does It Accelerate Return to Sport? 🏃‍♂️⏱️


A recent systematic review and meta-analysis (2024) examined treatment outcomes for acute Achilles tendon rupture (AATR). 📊 The study found that **early rehabilitation following surgical repair led to a significantly faster return to sport** compared to later rehabilitation (mean difference of -4.19 weeks; p = 0.002). 🔑


However, the **overall rate of return to sport was similar** between early and late rehabilitation groups. ⚖️


Additionally, functional outcomes, such as the **Achilles Tendon Total Rupture Score (ATRS)**, did not differ significantly between the two rehabilitation timelines. 📈


**Question:**


Given these findings, how do you approach rehabilitation timelines in your practice? 🩺


Do you prioritize **early rehab to speed up return to sport** 🏃‍♀️, or consider other factors to determine the **optimal rehab schedule**? 🤔
