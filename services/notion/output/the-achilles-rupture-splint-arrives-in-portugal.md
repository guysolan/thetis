## Content overview


The Achilles Rupture Splint Arrives in Portugal


Achilles tendon ruptures remain one of the most challenging injuries for athletes and active individuals alike. Re-rupture risk, wound complications, and slow rehabilitation timelines are just some of the hurdles clinicians face when guiding recovery.


That’s why we’re proud to share that the **Achilles Rupture Splint** is now officially available in **Portugal** — making it our **10th country**.


This milestone is possible thanks to our collaboration with [**OrthoDirect Portugal**](https://www.linkedin.com/company/orthodirect.pt/), who are committed to bringing innovative, evidence-based solutions to clinicians and patients.


The splint is designed to support safer, faster recovery by offering:

- 🛡️ Ensuring patients wear something to prevent re-ruptures at night
- 😴 Enhanced comfort during immobilization for better sleeping
- 🛌 Improved hygiene as patients no longer need to wear a boot in bed

We look forward to seeing how clinicians in Portugal will integrate the Achilles Rupture Splint into their care pathways — helping patients not only return, but rebound.


When introducing new rehab tools, do you think about **patient comfort** as well as **clinical outcomes**?
