Long-Term Achilles Repair Outcomes—Here’s the Data 🔍


A systematic review covering 806 surgical cases tracked outcomes over at least 5 years post-repair. Key long-term stats:


– Re-rupture rate: 4.2%
– Revision surgery: 3.7%
– Deep vein thrombosis: 6.3%
– Wound infections: 3.6%


These results highlight that surgical Achilles repair offers durable long-term success, with low re-rupture and revision rates—and manageable complication risks.
