## Understanding Your Options

**Surgery vs. Non-Surgical:**
Both paths can work. The choice often depends on:

- Your tendon gap size and whether it closes when your foot is pointed down
- Your age, activity level, and preferences
- Your surgeon's experience and protocol

The key is following the right protocol for whichever path you choose.

## Key Questions to Understand Your Plan

Make sure you understand:

- **What is my tendon gap size?** Does it close with toes pointed down? What does that mean for my treatment?
- **What protocol are you following?** Boot angles, timeline, weight-bearing progression â€” get the details
- **When do I start physio?** What are the early goals and milestones?

## The Importance of Protocol

Whether you go surgical or non-surgical, following the right protocol matters. The boot angles, weight-bearing progression, and physio timing all need to be coordinated properly.
