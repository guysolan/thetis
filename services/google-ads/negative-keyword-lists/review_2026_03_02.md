# Negative Keyword Categorization & Best Practices

This directory manages our negative keyword lists for Google Ads. We optimize for:
- **Achilles Recovery Course**: 31 expert lessons, rehab roadmaps, and exercises.
- **Thetis Night Splint**: Lightweight protection for sleeping during Achilles rupture recovery (first 12 weeks).

---

## 🛑 Deletions: Keywords We Are Now Targeting

We have removed the following keywords from our negative lists because they represent our core target audience for the **Course** and **Splint**.

### 1. Rehab & Exercise (Now Target: Recovery Course)
- **Deleted**: `achilles rehab exercises`, `achilles strengthening`, `achilles mobility exercises`, `achilles exercise routine`, `rehabilitation exercises`, `stretching`, `exercises`
- **Reason**: These users are looking for exactly what our Course provides. We want to be their guide.

### 2. Post-Op & Surgery (Now Target: Night Splint & Course)
- **Deleted**: `achilles surgery recovery`, `rupture surgery`, `achilles operation recovery`, `achilles tendon surgery`, `achilles surgery`, `ankle surgery`
- **Reason**: Post-op patients are the #1 buyers of the Night Splint (to replace the heavy boot at night) and the Course (to navigate the complex post-op timeline).

### 3. Boot & Brace (Now Target: Night Splint)
- **Deleted**: `walking boot achilles tendon`, `achilles walking boot`, `achilles wedge boot`, `achilles boot wedge removal`, `achilles support brace`, `achilles tendon support`, `ankle brace`
- **Reason**: We solve the "sleeping in a boot" problem. These users are our primary sales funnel for the splint.

### 4. Diagnosis & Pain (Now Target: Early-Stage Course)
- **Deleted**: `achilles pain`, `achilles tendon symptoms`, `achilles rupture symptoms`, `thompson test`, `achilles injury stages`, `achilles tear test`, `how to fix`, `how to heal your achilles`
- **Reason**: These queries represent users at the beginning of their journey. We want to capture them with the Course to prevent common mistakes like tendon elongation.

## 🧠 Best Practice Examples & Corrections

The following 50 keywords have been audited and updated to ensure they use the most efficient match type for Google Ads.

### 1. Achilles Tendinopathy (`achilles_tendinopathy.csv`)

### tendonitis
**BROAD -> PHRASE**
- **Why?**: The course is "Not for tendonitis." However, BROAD is too aggressive and might block valid rupture queries. PHRASE blocks the specific intent while allowing combinations.

### achilles massage
**BROAD -> PHRASE**
- **Why?**: Targets the service intent (massage therapy) while keeping the door open for product-related overlaps.

### running shoes for achilles
**BROAD -> PHRASE**
- **Why?**: They are looking for shoes, not splints/courses. PHRASE captures this specific category exclusion.

### achilles tendon anatomy
**BROAD -> PHRASE**
- **Why?**: Blocks the student/researcher intent without blocking a buyer who happens to use these words.

### 5. General Keywords (`general_keywords.csv`)

### free
**PHRASE -> BROAD**
- **Why?**: E-commerce best practice. "Free" traffic rarely converts for premium medical products.

### images
**PHRASE -> BROAD**
- **Why?**: "Images" and "Videos" denote researchers, not buyers. BROAD cleans this up across all queries.

### cheap
**EXACT -> BROAD**
- **Why?**: We are a premium solution. BROAD `cheap` removes value-seekers who won't convert at our price point.

**PHRASE -> EXACT**
- **Why?**: "Reviews" can be a buyer keyword (e.g., "Thetis splint reviews"). EXACT `[reviews]` blocks the generic query but keeps our brand reviews visible.

### diy
**PHRASE -> BROAD**
- **Why?**: DIYers are not buyers for medical splints. BROAD excludes them entirely.

## 🛍 Marketplace Audit: Shopify-First vs Amazon

We have added a new set of **Marketplace Negatives** to our `general_keywords.txt` file to ensure we don't pay for clicks from users who have already decided to buy from Amazon or other marketplaces.

### 1. High-Volume Marketplace Negatives
- **Added**: `amazon`, `prime`, `fba`, `temu`, `etsy`
- **Reason**: These users are looking for a specific marketplace experience (e.g., Prime shipping). We want them to find us organically on Amazon if they already have "Prime Brain," but we don't want to pay for a click that will likely bounce once they realize it's a direct Shopify site.

### 2. Marketplace Intent Negatives
- **Added**: `"on amazon"`, `"amazon reviews"`, `"ali express"`
- **Reason**: These phrases signal that the user is deep in the Amazon or marketplace ecosystem. They are looking for a specific type of research (Amazon reviews) or a specific type of marketplace (AliExpress).
