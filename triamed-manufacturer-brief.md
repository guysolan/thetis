# Thetis Medical - Product Information for Triamed

Thank you for asking for more information.

The product is the **Thetis Medical Achilles Tendon Night Splint**. It is a lightweight medical splint used by patients recovering from an Achilles tendon rupture. It is worn when resting or sleeping, instead of wearing a large walking boot.

We are looking for a manufacturing partner who may be able to help assemble the product. If you can also offer storage / 3PL dispatch, that would be useful, but it is not essential.

## Product Summary

- Brand: Thetis Medical
- Product: Achilles Tendon Night Splint
- Main shell: polypropylene (PP) plastic
- Sizes: Small and Large
- Sides: Left and Right
- Total versions: 4
- Packaging: zip-lock bag with IFU booklet
- Label: barcode, CE/UKCA, size, side and batch number

## What Thetis Can Supply

We can supply:

- PP plastic shells, at least initially
- Sample finished products
- Assembly instructions with photos / drawings
- IFU booklets in English, German, Spanish, French and Italian
- Label artwork and barcode details

The PP shell is currently made by another manufacturer in the UK. Later, if the project works well, we may be able to move the mould tool to Triamed or to a local supplier.

## Parts We Would Like Triamed To Source

We would like to understand whether Triamed could source and assemble:

- 50 mm webbing
- Spacer fabric
- 25 mm elastic
- 40 mm silicone-backed elastic
- Velcro / hook-and-loop
- Zip-lock bags
- Printed labels from Thetis artwork

We can provide specifications, or we can review suitable alternatives proposed by Triamed.

## Assembly

Each finished unit would include:

- 1 assembled splint
- 1 IFU booklet
- 1 zip-lock bag
- 1 product label

There is no retail box planned at the moment.

We are trying to keep the manufacturing process simple and cost-effective. Any advice from Triamed would be very welcome. We have already been advised that it may be better:

- Not to sew the spacer fabric through the plastic shell
- Not to sew the velcro on top of the nylon / webbing

We would welcome your view on the easiest, strongest and most economical way to attach these parts.

This 3PL service is optional. The main question is whether Triamed could help manufacture / assemble the product.
