## Campaign Structure Recommendations

### Ad Groups

1. **Surgery/Repair Keywords** - For Campaign 1
2. **Treatment/Clinical Keywords** - For Campaign 2
3. **Diagnostic Keywords** - Shared, lower priority
4. **Educational/Research Keywords** - Shared, lowest priority

### Ad Copy Suggestions

**Campaign 1 (Splint Affiliate)**:

- Headline: "Achilles Splint Affiliate Program"
- Description: "Help patients sleep comfortably without boot. Join our affiliate program for achilles rupture splints."
- CTA: "Become an Affiliate"

**Campaign 2 (Free Poster)**:

- Headline: "Free Achilles Recovery Poster & Kit Guide"
- Description: "Complete recovery kit checklist poster. Reduce repetitive patient explanations. Free for clinicians."
- CTA: "Get Free Poster"

### Geographic Targeting

- **UK**: All regions
- **US**: All states
- Consider excluding non-English speaking regions if budget is limited

### Bid Strategy

- Start with manual CPC
- Use enhanced CPC for optimization
- Set max CPC limits based on keyword bid ranges
- Monitor and adjust based on conversion data

### Negative Keywords

- Add patient-focused terms: "recovery", "pain", "symptoms" (unless specifically needed)
- Add "reddit", "forum", "discussion"
- Add "surgery cost", "surgery price"

## Total Campaign Budget

- **Campaign 1 (Splint Affiliate)**: £1,200-£1,500/month
- **Campaign 2 (Free Poster)**: £2,000-£2,500/month
- **Total Monthly Budget**: £3,200-£4,000
- **Total Expected Conversions**: 260-325/month
- **Average CPA**: £12-£15 (blended)

## Implementation Notes

1. Start with Campaign 2 (free offer) as it has higher conversion potential
2. Use search term reports to identify additional relevant keywords
3. Create separate campaigns for UK and US if significant performance differences emerge
4. Monitor Quality Score and adjust ad relevance
5. A/B test ad copy focusing on clinician pain points
6. Consider remarketing for clinicians who visit but don't convert
7. Track conversions at both landing page and form submission levels
