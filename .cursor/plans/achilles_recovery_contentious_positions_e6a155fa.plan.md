# Contentious Aspects of Achilles Rupture Recovery - Position Options

Based on review of all recovery phase articles and web research, here are the debatable aspects that need consistent positions:

## 1. Surgery vs Non-Surgical Treatment

**Question:** When is surgery required vs when is non-surgical treatment appropriate?

**A.** Surgery is required for all ruptures

**B.** Non-surgical treatment is appropriate for all ruptures

**C.** It depends on the patient (gap size, activity level, age, etc.)

**D.** Non-surgical is first-line; surgery only for specific indications (large gap, elite athletes, etc.)

**Current article stance:** D (non-surgical first-line with surgery for specific cases)

---

**C.** Both are equivalent - patient compliance matters more than brand

**D.** VACOped is better but cost/availability makes Aircast acceptable
